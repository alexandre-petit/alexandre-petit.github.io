About the Project

This project is based on the diamond dataset (link). The goal is to build a fast diamond price prediction model with information about the carat, diamond quality and color, geometry

I decide to ignore the x, y, z columns because we already have information about the carat, the table and depth.

I tried a few models, such as linear regression, decision tree, gradient boosting and XGBoost.

To further improve the results, I applied data standardization and target transform method. Because I faced some issues between XGBoost and some sklearn function, I did these two last processes on sklearn gradient boosting regressor, despite results not as good as those obtained with XGBoost.

XGBoost performs better that the Scaled and Transform Gradient Boosting, however I have not try to tune the hyperparameter since the results were satisfying. (9.7% mean absolute error, 0.97 r2).
Further improvement could be made by doing a GridSearch on the power transform and the log transform models to find the ideal hyperparameters.
